Design Informatics Project: Explorable Scrollytelling

Introduction

Wang, Sundin, Murray-Rust and Bach (2020) developed a series of cheatsheets for supporting data visualisation literacy, which cover topics such as the construction, visual patterns, and pitfalls of a series of visualisation types that are not obvious to interpret. They state that investigating how cheatsheets could work in conjunction with other formats or be expressed in different formats is a natural next step in the investigation, as varying formats support different approaches to learning and can be grouped to provide a more wholesome learning experience. This project focuses on the format of interactive visualisations, exploring how the information contained in cheatsheets could be translated and enhanced by interactive components that are presented in a readable narrative flow on a webpage, and what an authoring tool for assembling and sharing these interactive webpages would look like.
